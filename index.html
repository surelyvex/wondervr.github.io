// 01 — About

The Studio

EST. 2026

PARALLAX STUDIOS is an independent VR game development studio founded in 2026 by VELZ and VEX — building immersive, high-quality experiences for the Meta Quest platform.

We specialize in multiplayer VR environments, first-person horror, and physics-driven social gameplay. Every title we ship uses Gorilla Tag-style locomotion — arm-swinging movement that puts the player's body at the center of every interaction. Custom networking, hand-built maps, and original systems designed specifically for the constraints and possibilities of standalone VR.

Small team. Big worlds. No compromises.

// 03 — Technology

Our Stack

CORE TOOLS
Unity Engine
Primary development environment for all VR titles. Built-in render pipeline, C# scripting, XR interaction toolkit, and custom shader work.
Photon PUN2
Real-time multiplayer networking for all online modes. Custom Photon event codes, room management, and Photon Voice 2 integration.
Meta Quest SDK
OVRInput, hand tracking, spatial audio, and Quest-specific optimizations — including APK builds for sideloading and Meta Store submission.
Blender
3D modeling, rigging, and environment asset creation. All geometry and custom props built in-house and optimized for Quest hardware limits.
C# / Custom Systems
Admin panels, moderation tools, GameModeManager, broadcast systems, and teleport RPC events — all under the LethalApeR namespace.
