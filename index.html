About
Liminal horror in virtual reality

Wonder. VR is a free VR horror game by Chronicle Studios LLC for Meta Quest. You awaken in a place that shouldn't exist — endless corridors, damp carpet, flickering fluorescent lights.

Explore ever-changing levels inspired by backrooms legends. Each room feels familiar, yet disturbingly wrong. Survive entities, uncover clues, and try to escape.

ENTITY 003EXTREME

The Smiler

"The Grinning Darkness"
Aggression
Speed
Stealth

Lurks in darkness. Two glowing eyes and a luminescent grin. Manipulates light itself — fluorescents die in its presence. Found in Level 0 and Level Fun.

Survival Protocol

Stay in lit areas. Never stare at the grin. If lights flicker, retreat immediately. Running in darkness is fatal.

ENTITY 010EXTREME

The Skinwalker

"The Impersonator"
Aggression
Speed
Deception

Polymorphic predator that assumes victim appearance. Inherits memories and mannerisms. Nearly indistinguishable from real players. Found in Level 0 and The Habitable Zone.

Survival Protocol

Trust no one unverified. Watch for repeated phrases. Attracted to blood. If someone approaches too eagerly, assume hostile.
